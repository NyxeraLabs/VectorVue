## 6. Pre-Engagement & RoE Checklist

**Engagement Name:** [Project Name]

**Primary Client Contact:** [Name/Email/Phone]

### I. Administrative & Legal (Must be signed/finalized)

* [ ] **MSA/SOW:** Master Service Agreement and Statement of Work signed.
* [ ] **Permission to Audit:** Explicit written "Get Out of Jail Free" card signed by an authorized officer.
* [ ] **Insurance:** Professional liability insurance (Errors & Omissions) is active.
* [ ] **Emergency Contact:** 24/7 technical contact for the client provided (in case of system instability).

### II. Technical Scope (Rules of Engagement)

* [ ] **Target List:** Finalized list of IPs, URLs, and Mobile App Bundle IDs.
* [ ] **Exclusion List:** Explicitly defined "Out of Bounds" assets (e.g., third-party SaaS, specific production servers).
* [ ] **Testing Windows:** Defined times (e.g., "Business hours only" vs. "After hours/Weekends").
* [ ] **Data Handling:** Agreement on how sensitive findings (passwords/PII) will be transmitted (e.g., encrypted PGP email or secure portal).

### III. Execution Boundaries

* [ ] **Social Engineering:** Is Phishing allowed? (Yes/No). If yes, are high-level executives off-limits?
* [ ] **Physical Security:** Is office tailgating allowed? (Yes/No).
* [ ] **Exploitation Level:** Is the goal "Proof of Concept" (stopping at the door) or "Post-Exploitation" (pivoting and data exfiltration)?
* [ ] **Denial of Service (DoS):** Are stress tests or buffer overflows that may crash services permitted? (Usually No).
